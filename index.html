Welcome.

If you're new to Game of Thrones or simply lost, you're at the right place. The biggest gripe some people have when starting this show is that it is difficult to follow.

It's expected - the family tree is massive and there is a long history. You could open wikipedia and read pages of text, but I have created this site to prepare you for the show in a visually appealing way.

Yes, this is spoiler-free, in case you were worried. Nothing from the show is given away, just what you should know before jumping into season one. If you're ready, keep scrolling.

If you didn't know already...

Game of Thrones is an adaptation of the series of fantasy novels known as A Song of Ice and Fire, written by George R. R. Martin. Easy - let's move on.

Where at?

Majority of the show takes place in a continent known as Westeros. On this magical land is a realm known as the Seven Kingdoms, which despite its name, is actually nine regions. Some stuff went down resulting in the nine there are now, but we don't need to delve into that.




The Seven Kingdoms

For each region, there are many houses (interpret them as families), but only one of those houses hold power over their respective region - they are called the Great Houses. So, with nine regions come nine Great Houses. The nine regions in The Seven Kindgoms are below:

The North
The Iron Islands
The Riverlands
The Vale of Arryn
The Westerlands
The Crownlands
The Reach
The Stormlands
Dorne

We'll get to the Great Houses for those regions in a bit.

The Iron Throne

The ruler of the Seven Kingdoms sits on the Iron Throne and holds power over everyone. The Iron Throne is located in King's Landing, the capital of the Seven Kingdoms. The house of whomever is the King (called the Royal House) therefore has large influence over everything imaginable. You can now understand why the battle for the Iron Throne is so important - all houses want that power and influence.



The Houses

While there are hundreds of houses in the Seven Kingdoms, there are a select few that are the focus of the show, and we will be looking at those only. You can hover over each shield/sigil to get a brief overview of the houses.



House Baratheon

They are the Great House of The Stormlands, and their leader is Robert Baratheon - the King. Thus, House Baratheon is the Royal House and possesses the most power. As the Royal House, they are also the Great House of The Crownlands by default. Storm's End is a castle in The Stormlands and the seat of House Baratheon.
House Arryn

They are the Great House of The Vale of Arryn, and their leader is Jon Arryn. He is also the Hand of the King. Meaning, he advises the King in decisions made and holds the 2nd most powerful position in the Seven Kingdoms. The Eyrie is a castle in The Vale of Arryn and the seat of House Arryn.
House Bolton

They are one of the houses in The North, and their leader is Roose Bolton. They are known for flaying their enemies (removing layers of their skin) and letting them rot to their death. See: their sigil. The Dreadfort is a castle in The North and the seat of House Bolton.
House Frey

They are one of the houses in The Riverlands, and their leader is Walder Frey. The Twins is a castle in The Riverlands and the seat of House Frey.
House Greyjoy

They are the Great House of The Iron Islands, and their leader is Balon Greyjoy. Pyke is a castle located on Pyke Island, one of The Iron Islands, and is the seat of House Greyjoy.
House Lannister

They are the Great House of The Westerlands, and their leader is Tywin Lannister. They are one of the oldest and richest houses in the Seven Kingdoms. Casterly Rock is a castle in The Westerlands and the seat of House Lannister.


House Martell

They are the Great House of Dorne, and their leader is Doran Martell. Sunspear is a castle in Dorne and the seat of House Martell.
House Tyrell

They are the Great House of The Reach, and their leader is Mace Tyrell. Highgarden is a castle in The Reach and the seat of House Tyrell.
House Stark

They are the Great House of The North, and their leader is Eddard Stark. They are one of the oldest houses in the Seven Kingdoms. Winterfell is a castle in The North and the seat of House Stark.
House Targaryen

They were the former Royal House of the Seven Kingdoms and thus the Great House of The Crownlands for nearly 300 years before the events of the show. Their leader is Daenerys Targaryen.
House Tully

They are the Great House of The Riverlands, and their leader is Edmure Tully. Riverrun is a castle in The Riverlands and the seat of House Tully.
